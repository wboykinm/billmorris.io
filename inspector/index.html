Bill Morris
Candidate for Inspector of Elections

ABOUT ME

I'm a husband, father, and lifelong Vermonter. I've lived and worked in Burlington's South End for 8 years now. In that time, I've seen the warmth and power of our community, and I've started to play a role in strengthening the institutions on which we depend.

For the past two years I've served as an Elections Official in Ward 5, and now I'd like to take on additional responsibilities as an Inspector of Elections.

PLATFORM

I'm running as an independent candidate for what I believe is essentially a nonpartisan position. My view is that voting should be simple and broadly accessible, and that our city, state, and country are at their best when voter engagement and participation are at their highest.

Having counted the mailed-in and dropped-off ballots of the Covid era - having seen the clear increase in voter participation, even for off-cycle ballots - I am a strong supporter of universal mail-in voting. At this critical moment for our democratic processes nationally, I also reject all forms of voter suppression and disenfranchisement.

I hope to have your support in aiding the crucial work of free and fair elections in Ward 5. Please drop me a line if you have any questions.
